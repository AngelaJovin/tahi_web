    About Us

    Get to know us

    TAHI is a Non-Government Organization based on Tanzania mainland, a professional body that supports and represents the movement for conservation, protection and development of our heritage and enhance its veracity for present and future generation.

    TAHI advocates for value of caring and appreciating of heritage at all levels in the community. We bring people together of different professional with a passion for conserving our heritage.

    TAHI raises awareness on the importance of conserving, protecting and developing our heritage and promote sustainable conservation.

    Heritage conservation involves protection of things of value for present and future. It involves safeguarding of heritage in all of its forms (culture and natural). This involves Archaeology, Artworks, Wildlife, Forests, Traditions, Water Bodies, Architecture and other Social History Collections held in Museums.
